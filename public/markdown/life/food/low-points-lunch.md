> **Bällchen** comes from _Ball_ (ball) + _-chen_ (diminutive), meaning “little ball”.  
> Commonly used for small meatballs, dumplings, or other bite-sized rounds.

Light, lean turkey meatballs designed to keep points low without sacrificing flavour.
